ACME Store — Customer Support Knowledge Base
============================================

SECTION 1: RETURNS AND REFUNDS
-------------------------------

Return Policy
We accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by a valid receipt or order confirmation email. Damaged, opened, or used items are not eligible for return unless they arrived defective.

How to Initiate a Return
To start a return, visit our Returns Portal at www.acmestore.com/returns and enter your order number and email address. You will receive a prepaid return shipping label by email within 24 hours. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any authorised courier location.

Refund Processing
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank. Store credit refunds are applied immediately.

Exchanges
If you received a defective or incorrect item, we will exchange it at no additional cost. Contact our support team with your order number and a photo of the issue and we will arrange a replacement shipment.

Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned: digital downloads, gift cards, personalised or custom-made products, perishable goods, and items marked as final sale at the time of purchase.


SECTION 2: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
----------------------------------

Standard Shipping
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days and is free on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 incur a flat $4.99 shipping fee. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.

Express Shipping
Express shipping (2–3 business days) is available for $12.99. Same-day delivery is available in select cities for $19.99 on orders placed before 12:00 PM local time.

International Shipping
We ship to over 50 countries. International orders typically arrive within 10–21 business days. Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are the responsibility of the recipient and vary by country.

Order Tracking
You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order has been dispatched. Track your order at www.acmestore.com/track or directly on the carrier's website. Tracking information may take up to 12 hours to update after dispatch.

Lost or Delayed Packages
If your package has not arrived within 3 business days of the estimated delivery date, please contact our support team. We will open an investigation with the carrier. If the package is confirmed lost, we will reship or refund your order at no charge.


SECTION 3: PAYMENT METHODS
----------------------------

Accepted Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit cards
- PayPal and PayPal Credit
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- ACME Store Gift Cards
- Bank transfer (for orders over $500)

Payment Security
All transactions are encrypted using TLS 1.3. We do not store your full card number on our servers. Payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processor.

Billing Issues
If your payment is declined, check that your billing address matches the one on file with your bank and that your card has not expired. If the problem persists, contact your bank or try a different payment method. For any unauthorised charges, contact us immediately and we will investigate.

Instalment Plans
Orders over $100 are eligible for buy-now-pay-later through Klarna or Afterpay. Select your preferred option at checkout and you will be redirected to complete the application. Approval is subject to the provider's terms.

Invoices
A receipt is emailed automatically after every purchase. To request a VAT invoice or a detailed billing statement, email billing@acmestore.com with your order number.


SECTION 4: ACCOUNT AND LOGIN
------------------------------

Creating an Account
You can create a free account at www.acmestore.com/register. An account allows you to track orders, save your address and payment details, and access your order history. Guest checkout is also available if you prefer not to register.

Forgotten Password
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes. Check your spam folder if it does not arrive. If you still cannot access your account, contact support.

Changing Account Details
Log in and go to My Account > Profile Settings to update your name, email address, phone number, or password. Changes to your email address will require verification via a confirmation link sent to the new address.

Deleting Your Account
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email privacy@acmestore.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process the request within 30 days in compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Pending orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion.


SECTION 5: PRODUCTS AND WARRANTY
----------------------------------

Product Information
Detailed specifications, dimensions, materials, and care instructions are listed on each product page. If you need additional information not shown on the page, use the "Ask a Question" feature on the product page or contact our support team.

Warranty Coverage
Most products come with a 12-month manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Some product categories carry extended warranties of 24 or 36 months — check the product page for details. Warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorised modifications.

Filing a Warranty Claim
Email warranty@acmestore.com with your order number, a description of the defect, and clear photographs. We will respond within 2 business days with instructions for repair, replacement, or refund depending on the situation.

Product Recalls
We follow all mandatory recall notices issued by relevant safety authorities. If a product you purchased is subject to a recall, we will contact you directly by email and provide instructions for a free return and full refund.

SECTION 7: PROMOTIONS AND LOYALTY PROGRAMME
---------------------------------------------

Discount Codes
Promotional codes can be entered at checkout in the "Promo Code" field. Codes are case-insensitive and can only be used once per customer unless stated otherwise. Codes cannot be combined with other offers or applied to previous orders.

ACME Rewards Programme
Earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed at a rate of 100 points = $1 off your next order. Sign up at www.acmestore.com/rewards. Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.

Price Match Guarantee
If you find the same item cheaper at an authorised retailer within 14 days of your purchase, we will match the price and refund the difference. Submit your price match request at www.acmestore.com/pricematch with a link to the competing offer.

Newsletter Subscribers
Subscribe to our newsletter to receive a 10% discount on your first order, early access to sales, and exclusive member-only offers. Unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link at the bottom of any email.
